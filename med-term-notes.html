Medical Terminology Notes

Parts

Prefixes

  • electro- = using electicity
  • hypo- = below average
  • peri- = around

Roots

  • colo = colon
  • cardi(o) = heart
  • pharygn = throat

Suffixes

  • -scopy = create an opening
  • -gram = a picture
  • -itis = inflammation

Examples

  • colonoscopy
  • electrocardiogram
  • pharygngitis
  • hypopharyngeal
  • pericarditis
  • pharyngitis
